Mission

Spreading the Passion of Programming

We're a bunch of students with the common interest of computer programming, 3D modeling, or computer science in general. Instead of programming locked up in our basements with the curtains drawn and lights off, we get together at NNHS to collaborate on projects and further understanding. Most importantly, we want to give NNHS students the opportunity to pursue their CS interests. We have members who've been programming for years, and others for a few days. No matter where you are, we'll have a spot for you.

Released Projects

Tiger Insider Website
4+ months development time

The Tiger Insider website uses the bootstrap CSS framework and silverstripe PHP for the backend. The club created some graphics to slap on the website and hired a gfx artist online to create an article placeholder banner. We like to use our own code, so bootstrap CSS was the optimal way in efficiency and creativity. Of course, like any programming project, we had our share of keyboard-smashing moments.

3D OOP Lua game
12+ months development Played 100,000+ Times 1,600$+ in sales

We won't mention the name of this game, partly to protect our anonymity if any of our players find out about this website. It's an action combat multiplayer fighting game. We used an object-oriented version of Lua and blender. The physics engine used was roblox studio. The game is now released on the roblox platform. Over the course development period, we hired various gfx artists and 3D modelers online to create art assets.

Orbit - A Space Game
3+ months development Downloaded 10,000 Times

This is one of our more known projects by NNHS students. It's a neat scroller app game to play when class gets boring. We used swift/xcode and even made the music with garage band. It's now released on the app store.
